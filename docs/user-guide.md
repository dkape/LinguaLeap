# LinguaLeap User Guide

This guide explains how to use LinguaLeap for both teachers and students.

### Getting Started

1. **Register as a Teacher**
   - Visit `/de/signup/teacher` (German) or `/en/signup/teacher` (English)
   - Fill in your name, email, and password
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Click the verification link to activate your account

2. **First Login**
   - Visit `/de/login/teacher` or `/en/login/teacher`
   - Enter your verified email and password
   - You'll be redirected to your teacher dashboard

### Class Management

#### Creating Student Classes

1. Navigate to the **"Klassen" (Classes)** tab in your dashboard
2. Click **"Neue Klasse" (New Class)**
3. Fill in the class details:
   - **Name**: e.g., "Klasse 3A" or "Grade 3A"
   - **Description**: Brief description of the class
   - **Language**: German or English
   - **Age Range**: Select appropriate age group (5-6, 7-8, 9-10, 11-12 years)
4. Click **"Klasse erstellen" (Create Class)**

### Creating AI-Powered Challenges

#### Challenge Generation Process

1. Go to the **"Herausforderungen" (Challenges)** tab
2. Fill in the challenge configuration:
   - **Topic**: e.g., "Das Sonnensystem", "Dinosaurier", "M√§rchen"
   - **Class Description**: Describe your students to help AI tailor content
   - **Age Range**: Select the appropriate age group
   - **Reading Level**: Choose beginner, intermediate, or advanced
   - **Language**: German or English
   - **Assign to Class**: Optionally assign to a specific class

3. Click **"Herausforderung generieren" (Generate Challenge)**
4. Wait for AI to create the challenge (usually 30-60 seconds)
5. Review the generated content:
   - Reading passages from public domain sources
   - Comprehension quizzes with multiple-choice questions
   - Points allocation and time estimates

6. Click **"Herausforderung speichern" (Save Challenge)** to make it available to students

#### Challenge Content Features

- **Public Domain Sources**: Content sourced from Project Gutenberg and other child-safe sources
- **Age-Appropriate**: Content automatically adapted to selected age range
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Alternating text passages and quizzes
- **Source Attribution**: Proper references to original books and authors
- **Safety Filtering**: All content is child-safe and educationally appropriate

## üéì For Students

### Getting Started

1. **Register as a Student**
   - Visit `/de/signup/student` (German) or `/en/signup/student` (English)
   - Fill in your name, email, and password
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Click the verification link to activate your account

2. **Join Classes**
   - Your teacher will add you to classes using your email address
   - You'll automatically see assigned challenges in your dashboard

### Completing Challenges

#### Starting a Challenge

1. Go to **"Meine Herausforderungen" (My Challenges)** tab
2. Find an available challenge (marked as "Nicht begonnen" - Not Started)
3. Click **"Starten" (Start)** to begin
4. The challenge will open in reading mode

#### Reading Process

1. **Read Text Passages**:
   - Read at your own pace
   - Click **"Weiter" (Go Ahead)** when ready to continue
   - Your reading speed is automatically tracked
   - Take your time to understand the content

2. **Complete Quizzes**:
   - Answer multiple-choice questions about what you read
   - Choose the best answer from 4 options (A, B, C, D)
   - Questions test comprehension and vocabulary
   - Each correct answer earns points

3. **Progress Through Items**:
   - Challenges alternate between reading passages and quizzes
   - Complete all items to finish the challenge
   - Your progress is saved automatically

#### Earning Points

- **Reading Completion**: Points for finishing text passages
- **Quiz Accuracy**: More points for correct answers
- **Speed Bonus**: Additional points for efficient completion
- **Comprehension**: Higher scores for better understanding

### Tracking Your Progress

#### Personal Statistics

View your performance in the dashboard:
- **Total Points**: All points earned across challenges
- **Completed Challenges**: Number of finished challenges
- **Average Time**: Your typical completion time
- **Current Rank**: Your position in class leaderboards

#### Leaderboard Competition

1. Go to **"Bestenliste" (Leaderboard)** tab
2. Select your class to view rankings
3. See how you compare to classmates:
   - **Points Ranking**: Total points earned
   - **Completion Count**: Challenges finished
   - **Speed Metrics**: Average completion time
   - **Accuracy Stats**: Quiz performance

#### Challenge Status Tracking

- **Not Started**: Available challenges you haven't begun
- **In Progress**: Challenges you've started but not finished
- **Completed**: Finished challenges with your scores
- **Progress Bars**: Visual indication of completion percentage

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### For Teachers

**Can't add students to class**:
- Ensure the student has registered with the exact email address
- Check that the student has verified their email
- Verify the student registered with "student" role, not "teacher"

**AI challenge generation fails**:
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different topic or simpler description
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Contact support if issues persist

#### For Students

**Can't see assigned challenges**:
- Confirm your teacher has added you to a class
- Check that challenges are assigned to your class
- Verify you're logged in with the correct account
- Refresh the page or try logging out and back in

**Challenge won't start**:
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Check if the challenge is still active
- Contact your teacher if problems continue

### Getting Help

- **Teacher Support**: Contact your system administrator
- **Student Support**: Ask your teacher for assistance
- **Technical Issues**: Check the troubleshooting sections in documentation
- **Account Problems**: Verify email verification was completed
